Scroll for fun. Guarenteed!!! We PROMISE!!

By Elmar Hjaltason

Social media is like the modern drug. It’s new, it’s fresh, shiny, and fun. With the creation of tik tok and youtube shorts, scrolling around online has become so much easier. Short Form content that we find satisfying to let rush through our mind endlessly.
It can get out of hand, however. I’ve personally managed to clock in an average of 4-5 hours on youtube a day, most of it from doom scrolling.
If we let it get out of hand, we’ll be forced to watch as our precious days fade away in an instant.
